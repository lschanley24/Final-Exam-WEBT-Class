Orgain Company

Orgain Protein Company is a leading provider of high-quality organic protein products designed to support healthy lifestyles and nutritional needs. Founded by Dr. Andrew Abraham, a physician and cancer survivor, Orgain was created out of a personal mission to make clean, nutritious protein accessible to everyone. The company prides itself on using 100% organic ingredients, ensuring that each product is free from artificial additives and GMOs. With a variety of delicious flavors and formats, including protein powders, shakes, and bars, Orgain caters to diverse dietary preferences, including vegan options. Their commitment to sustainability is reflected in their eco-friendly packaging and sourcing practices, which prioritize environmental responsibility. By focusing on nutrition and taste, Orgain Protein Company has established itself as a trusted brand for individuals seeking to enhance their health and wellness journey.
