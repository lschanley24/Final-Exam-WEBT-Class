About Orgain

Orgain was founded with a simple mission: to provide clean, nutritious, and delicious products for everyone. Our founder, Dr. Andrew Abraham, started this journey after facing his own health challenges. He recognized the need for organic nutritional solutions that could support a healthy lifestyle. Today, Orgain stands as a leader in the organic nutrition market, with a commitment to quality and innovation. We are passionate about helping individuals achieve their health goals through better nutrition. Our story is rooted in a desire to inspire healthier choices for a brighter future.

Our Mission

At Orgain, our mission is to elevate the health of individuals and communities through organic nutrition. We believe that what you put into your body matters, and we strive to create products that reflect this philosophy. Our commitment extends beyond just providing great lasting products; we aim to educate and empower our customers about health and wellness. Sustainability is at the core of our values, guiding our sourcing and production practices. We actively engage with communities to promote a healthier lifestyle and support local initiatives. Join us in our journey to transform the way people think about nutrition.

Commitment to Health

  • Researching and innovating for the highest nutritional standards
  • Meticulously selecting each ingredient for health benefits.
  • Providing transparency about our ingredients and sourcing.
  • Creating a supportive environment for informed nutritional choices.

Our Story

The story of Orgain is one of passion, dedication, and a commitment to health. Founded by Dr. Andrew Abraham, Orgain emerged from a personal journey towards better nutrition after facing health challenges. His vision was to create organic, nutritious products that could help others improve their well-being. Over the years, Orgain has grown into a trusted name in the organic nutrition space, driven by a team of experts who share the same commitment to quality and health. Our journey continues as we seek to innovate and inspire healthier eating habits for individuals and families everywhere. Together, we can make a difference in the world of nutrition.
